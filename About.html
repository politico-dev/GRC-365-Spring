    Hello there! I'm an artist who enjoys drawing edgy dogs of both feral and anthropomorphic varieties. I can also do humanoid characters and felines, but am willing to challenge myself to draw other things!

    In addition to drawing, I also do screenprinting and writing! Most of my current writing can be found on AO3 and Wattpad.

    I was also an editor for a small visual novel team called Simporiem Studios. Over the course of a few months, we put together a heartfelt, fan-made game dedicated to a Nijisanji EN vtuber group called Luxiem.
